Voice principles

  • One line, two answers — what am I looking at, and what should I do.
  • Verb + object — "Start an exploration", "Lower the star threshold", never "Get started!".
  • Name the mechanism — say what earned inclusion ("survived the red team"), what triggers a star (≥75 with earned confidence), what the governor is doing.
  • Hypotheses, not promises — every score is a prioritized guess with visible provenance.
  • Never apologize, never hype — no ":(", no "!", no "amazing".
