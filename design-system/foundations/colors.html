Color system

Every hue carries exactly one meaning. If you're reaching for color to decorate, use neutral instead.

Blue is the market, vermillion is you.Blue encodes market evidence — viability, demand, signals. Vermillion encodes the founder — fit, steering, "for you". Never mix the two.
