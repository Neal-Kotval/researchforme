Trust-encoded viability

A number's visual weight must match how much it deserves belief. The same 84, three ways — confidence is visible, not a label.

84
Corroborated

High + tool-corroborated. Earned by deep rigor plus independent tool corroboration (search hits, repo counts, pricing pages). Solid heavy numeral, solid ramp-4 border, full saturation.

84
Medium confidence

Medium. Standard-rigor pass, no independent corroboration yet. Normal weight, mid-ramp color, plain hairline border. Neither shouts nor apologizes.

84
unverified

Low / light rigor / uncorroborated. Dashed border, desaturated, smaller, with the explicit word "unverified". A light-rigor 93 must look weaker than a deep-rigor 61.

Tree-row chip equivalents (.viab-chip)
The same three trust levels at chip scale, plus the star variant for ideas that cleared the star threshold with earned confidence.

The law: saturation, weight, and border style encode belief; the numeral encodes magnitude. Never render an uncorroborated score with the solid treatment — a confident-looking guess is a lie.
